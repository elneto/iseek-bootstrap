About
The United Nations Intranet - iSeek was developed in 2005 to encourage knowledge-sharing throughout the UN system. Its mission statement is: One intranet for one UN worldwide, providing relevant and coherent messages to reach and connect staff everywhere, with consistent, user-friendly layout and standard technology.
